FAQ

Common questions

Does this actually eject water from a speaker?▾

Yes. Low frequency sound around 165Hz physically vibrates trapped water droplets out of the mesh — the same principle behind Apple Watch's built-in water eject shortcut.

Is it safe for my phone speaker?▾

At moderate volume, yes. The frequencies used are well within a speaker's normal operating range. Avoid maximum volume for extended sessions.

Does it work on Android too?▾

Yes, any device with a speaker and a modern browser works — iPhone, Android, iPad, or laptop.

Why isn't my speaker fixed after one try?▾

Heavier water exposure or compacted dust may need 2-3 repeat cycles. If sound stays muffled after multiple attempts, the speaker may need physical cleaning or repair.
