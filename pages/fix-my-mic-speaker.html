Clear a muffled microphone without touching your phone

If people on calls say your voice sounds distant or muffled, dust or lint in the tiny mic pinhole is the usual cause. This 800Hz-3kHz sweep helps dislodge it.

Keep volume moderate and hold your phone speaker-side down over a soft cloth. Stop immediately if you feel discomfort.
How to use it

Three quick steps

Locate the mic pinhole

Usually at the bottom edge near the charging port, or top edge near the camera.

Face the pinhole toward a speaker or cloth

This helps direct vibration into the small opening.

Run the sweep

Let the full 45 second sweep finish, then make a test call.

FAQ

Common questions

Why does my mic sound muffled but speaker sounds fine?▾

The mic pinhole is much smaller than the speaker mesh, so it clogs independently and more easily.

Does this fix software-related mic issues?▾

No, this only addresses physical blockage. Software issues need a settings check instead.

How do I know if it worked?▾

Record a short voice memo before and after, then compare clarity.
