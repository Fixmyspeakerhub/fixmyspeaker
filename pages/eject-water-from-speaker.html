Just got your phone wet? Clear it fast

Acting within the first few minutes matters most. This emergency-mode tool runs a fast 165Hz eject cycle so you can start clearing your speaker the moment water gets in.

How to use it

Three quick steps

Power through, don't panic

Keep the phone on if it's still responsive, this tool needs sound output to work.

Speaker down on cloth

Rest the speaker grille face-down on a towel to catch ejected droplets.

Run repeatedly

This shorter 30 second cycle is built to repeat quickly, run it 2-3 times back to back.
