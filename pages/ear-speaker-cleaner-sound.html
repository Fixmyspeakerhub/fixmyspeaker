FAQ

Common questions

Is the earpiece different from the main speaker?▾

Yes, it's smaller and used only for calls, not media playback, which is why it needs its own frequency range.

Why is only my call audio muffled?▾

This usually means dust is isolated to the earpiece grille, not the main bottom speaker.

Can I use the main speaker cleaner instead?▾

You can, but this dedicated range is more precisely tuned for the smaller earpiece opening.
