FAQ

Common questions

How long does water eject take?▾

One cycle runs 60 seconds. Most light exposure clears in a single run; heavier exposure may need two or three.

Can I use this right after my phone gets wet?▾

Yes, this is one of the best times to use it, before water has a chance to settle deeper into the mesh.

Will this damage a dry speaker?▾

No, playing a moderate 165Hz tone on a dry speaker is completely safe.
