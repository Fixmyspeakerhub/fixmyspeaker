FAQ

Common questions

Does this work through a case?▾

Silicone and most cases don't block sound output, but removing thick cases helps.

Is 165Hz safe for iPhone 13 speakers?▾

Yes, it's well within the speaker's normal frequency range and used at moderate volume.

What if only one speaker sounds affected?▾

Run the cycle twice, angling each speaker toward the cloth in turn.
