Clear dust and lint from your speaker

How to use it

Three quick steps

Tap the grille gently first

A light tap can dislodge loose surface dust before the sound cycle starts.

Face down on a hard surface

Unlike water, dust responds better over a hard flat surface rather than cloth.

Run the sweep

Let the full 45 second frequency sweep finish, then tap gently again.

FAQ

Common questions

How is this different from the water eject tool?▾

This uses a higher frequency range, 200-250Hz, better suited to dry particles than water droplets.

How often should I run this?▾

Monthly use prevents heavy buildup, especially if you keep your phone in a pocket often.

Will this fix a completely blocked speaker?▾

Severe blockage from months of buildup may need a soft brush alongside the sound cycle.
