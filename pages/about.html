About

Sound-based tools, built to be simple

FixMySpeaker is a small set of browser tools that use calibrated sound frequencies to clear water and dust from phone speakers. Every tool runs entirely in your browser using the Web Audio API — nothing is uploaded, stored, or tracked.

We built this after noticing how often a blocked or muffled speaker isn't actually broken — it just needs the right vibration to shake debris loose. No app store download, no permissions, no account. Open a tool, press play, and test your speaker.

Have a suggestion for a new tool or a device that needs its own tuned frequency? Get in touch.
