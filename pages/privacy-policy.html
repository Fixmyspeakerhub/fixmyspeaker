Privacy policy

FixMySpeaker does not collect personal data, require accounts, or upload anything from your device.

What we don't collect

No microphone access, no file uploads, no account creation, and no personal information is requested by any tool on this site.

Local processing only

All audio tools run using your browser's built-in Web Audio API. Sound is generated and played entirely on your device — nothing is sent to a server.

Analytics

This site may use basic, anonymized page-view analytics to understand which tools are most useful. No individual visitor is identified.
